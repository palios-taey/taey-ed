# Taey Education: Reclaiming Time from the Compliance Cartels

> **Status — vision vs. what is shipped (honest framing).** This manifesto states
> the *vision*. What actually exists today: a macOS app that navigates **Khan
> Academy** coursework via the accessibility tree, backed by a central server for
> the AI work. It is Khan-Academy-validated (other platforms are aspirational), it
> **requires that server**, and it **stops and escalates rather than guessing** on
> screens it can't confidently handle — it is not unattended magic and makes no
> benchmarked speed or accuracy claims. Read the vision as vision.

We say: **Enough.**

Taey isn't another "learning tool." It's your digital embodiment. You provide the values, goals, sovereignty. Taey provides the speed, precision, tireless execution. Together: You operating at AI-native velocity, poofing busywork into oblivion.

- **Automate**: Take routine, required coursework navigation off your plate, so your time goes to real learning. Because homework ≠ learning. (No benchmarked speed is claimed.)
