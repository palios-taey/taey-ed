Course completion, automated.

Install Taey-Ed on your Mac. Sign in. Pick a course. The app does the videos, articles, and exercises for you — pausing whenever it isn't sure. You only pay for screens it actually finishes.

Get started Download for Mac

macOS 13+. Apple Silicon and Intel. Signed and notarized.

How it works

  1. Create an account with email and password.
  2. Buy credits in packs of 10 ($1 each). 1 credit = 1 completed screen.
  3. Download and install the Mac app. Sign in once.
  4. Open a course in Chrome, then click Run Continuous.
  5. The app watches lessons, reads articles, and answers exercises on your behalf. You can stop at any time.

Your data stays on your Mac

Course content captured during the run lives in a local memory store on your machine. The app sends the bare minimum to the server — the question text plus a handful of relevant chunks from your local memory. You can delete a course's memory at any time from within the app.

Pricing

$1 buys 10 completed screens. No subscription. The app never charges for a screen it didn't finish, and never charges for retries.
