Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This policy explains what data Taey-Ed collects, what stays on your Mac, what crosses the network, and how you can delete it. It is written to match how the product actually works, not how a generic SaaS template works.

What stays on your Mac

The Taey-Ed Mac app captures content from the courses you run and stores it in a local memory database on your machine:

  • Full text of video transcripts and article bodies you watch / read while the app is running.
  • 4096-dimension semantic vectors generated from that content (used for local retrieval).
  • Cached chat history with the Taey-Ed backend.
  • Your access and refresh tokens (Keychain).

The full local memory lives under ~/Library/Application Support/taey-ed/kb/<course_id>/. You can delete a course's memory from inside the app at any time (Local course memory → Delete this course's memory). That removes both the text and the vectors for that course from your Mac. The full Keychain and chat cache can be cleared by removing the app.

What crosses the network

Three categories of data leave your Mac:

WhatWhere it goesWhy
Account email + password (hashed in transit via HTTPS) Taey-Ed backend So you can sign in.
Screen accessibility tree + screenshot for the page the app is currently on Taey-Ed backend So the app can decide what to do next on that page.
Page text to embed Taey-Ed backend's embedding service The backend returns a vector. The text is not retained on the backend; only the vector you get back is, and only transiently inside the request.
Top-K relevant chunks from your local memory Taey-Ed backend So the AI generating the next action has the relevant context to help. Only the small handful of chunks ranked most relevant to the current page are sent, never the entire local memory.
Payment information (card details) Stripe Stripe processes the charge. Taey-Ed never sees or stores your card number.

What we keep on our servers

  • Your account: email, salted hash of your password, creation timestamp, verification status.
  • Credit ledger: append-only history of purchases and successful-screen debits. This is required for billing accuracy and never deleted (except as legally required).
  • Refresh token hashes (we store hashes, not the tokens themselves; this lets us revoke a compromised token).
  • Operational logs of API calls. These do not include the content of your screens, your captured text, embeddings, or payment details. Tokens and screenshots are scrubbed before logging.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
  • We don't use your course content to train models.
  • We don't track you across the web with cookies. The only browser storage we use is localStorage for your authentication tokens and email on the marketing/account site.
  • We don't run analytics or fingerprinting libraries on this site.

Third parties

  • Stripe processes payments. Stripe's privacy policy applies to the data you give them at checkout (card details, billing address). We only see the Stripe session ID and event webhooks (whether a payment succeeded).
  • Resend sends transactional emails (account verification). Resend sees your email address.
  • Cloudflare sits in front of our API and site as a CDN / proxy. Cloudflare may collect request-level metadata (IP address, user agent, response time). They are GDPR/CCPA compliant.

Your choices

  • Delete a course's local memory from inside the app at any time. The chunks are gone from your Mac immediately.
  • Stop the running automation at any time via the Stop button. No more screens get processed; no more data leaves your Mac.
  • Sign out from the app or the website. Your refresh token is revoked.
  • Delete your account: email privacy@taey.ai. We will remove your account record. The credit ledger entries remain (we retain them as required for tax and billing records), but they will be detached from any identifying information.

Security

  • All transit is TLS (HTTPS) via Cloudflare's edge.
  • Account passwords are stored with a salted hash (Argon2 or equivalent strength).
  • Access JWTs are short-lived (~15 min) and rotated through a refresh-token flow.
  • Refresh tokens are stored on our server only as hashes.
  • On your Mac, the refresh token lives in macOS Keychain, which is encrypted by the operating system.

Children

This service is intended for users 18+ or for use under a parent's or legal guardian's supervision. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, contact privacy@taey.ai and we will remove the account.

Changes to this policy

We will update this policy as the product changes (more third-parties, new data flows, etc.). The “Last updated” date at the top will move. Material changes — meaning new categories of data collection or new categories of data sharing — will be emailed to your account email.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, or anything else: privacy@taey.ai
